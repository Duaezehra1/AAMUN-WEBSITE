Rules & Regulations

Violence, Harassment, and Discrimination
AAMUN is firmly committed to cultivating an inclusive and secure environment throughout the conference. We are dedicated to ensuring that every delegate, faculty advisor, and staff member feels respected and valued. Discrimination based on sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, or race, which could hinder anyone's complete engagement in any aspect of the conference, will not be tolerated.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Smoking
Delegates are kindly requested to note that smoking or vaping is prohibited for the duration of the conference as LUMS pushes its agenda to make it a smoke-free campus by the end of 2023.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dress code
Delegates are requested to observe Western formalwear throughout the duration of this conference. Note however, that the Chairs reserve the special right to enforce a dress code as per their requirement for the committee and the ability to deny a delegate entry into the committee session on violation of the stipulated dress code
